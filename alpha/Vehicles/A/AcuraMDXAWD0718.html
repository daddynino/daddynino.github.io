TOW INFORMATION 
The manufacturer has not approved a sling or wheel-lift procedure for this vehicle. Acura recommends the use of car carrier equipment with proper tie-down devices.
 
NOTE: 
If car carrier equipment is not available, research indicates that this vehicle may be towed on a wheel-lift with a tow dolly. Ensure clearance is maintained between the towing equipment and the undercarriage of the vehicle.
NOTE: 
An eyebolt is available for loading only. Eyebolt is stowed with tire tools under cargo area floor and threads into a receiver on the passenger's side of the front or rear bumper.
NOTE: 
Vehicle may be equipped with optional adjustable suspension making wheel strap tie down necessary when transporting on a car carrier. "T" hook slots are provided in the frame to secure a vehicle that is not equipped with air suspension or as attachment points for loading the vehicle
SHIFT OVERRIDE 
Located on the shifter housing next to "P" indicator on 2007-2013 models. 2014 and newer models do not have a shift override.
E-BRAKE OVERRIDE 
N/A
 
SERVICE INFORMATION 
JUMP START 
The battery is located under the hook on the drivers side. Follow normal jump-starting precautions.
TIRE CHANGING 
A spare tire stored under rear of vehicle. 2014 and newer modes may be equipped with an inflator kit which is stored under the rear cargo area floor on the passengers side. Follow the instructions on the kit to ensure proper installation of the sealant.
FUEL DELIVERY 
Fuel door override is located inside fuse box in the rear cargo area behind removable cover on the drivers side. 2017 and newer models may have a no-cap fuel system. A funnel for fueling is located in the cargo area.
ELECTRONIC KEYS 
Some models will be equipped with push button start. If the vehicle does not recognize the key hold the key fob with the Acura logo facing you, next to the START/STOP button. Depress the brake pedal and attempt to start the vehicle.
